FIRE PREVENTION MONTH - the month of March was named as the Fire Prevention Month due to how many fires happened across the country during that time. It is a good time to raise awareness about how fires start and how to prevent them from starting.
FIRE PREVENTION TIPS
MAINTAIN YOUR WIRES Don't put your wires near anything that could catch fire, like a carpet. Wires can get hot so there is a big possiblity.
STORE ANY FLAMMABLE OBJECTS CORRECTLY Place these away from any heaters and such. Items like rubbing alcohol, nail polish remover, etc. can easily catch fire if you aren't careful.
UNPLUG ITEMS YOU'RE NOT USING By doing this, you're not only preventing a fire but also saving electricity. Items like toasters or hair straighteners might overheat and cause a fire, even if it's not being used while plugged.
BE WARY AROUND OPEN FLAMES Be careful, someone might accidentally cause a fire just by walking a bit. Try to keep any candles away from places any children or pets can get, or anywhere anyone can push over.
